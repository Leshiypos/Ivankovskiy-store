Delivery
& Returns

Delivery

  • We carefully prepare each order to ensure it arrives in perfect condition — as beautiful as the moment it was crafted.

  • Shipping options:

    – Standard delivery (Free) – 3–7 business days

    – Express delivery – 1–3 business days (price calculated at checkout)

    – Worldwide shipping available

  • Tracking:

    Once your order is dispatched, you’ll receive a tracking number via email.

  • Packaging:

    All jewelry comes in signature Ivankovskiy boxes, ideal for gifting or safekeeping.

Returns

  • If for any reason you’re not fully satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. Items can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of delivery.

  • Conditions:

    – Jewelry must be unworn and in original condition

    – Original packaging must be included

    – Custom-made and engraved items are non-returnable

  • How to return:

    Contact us at support@ivankovskiy.com with your order number. We’ll guide you through the simple return process.

  • Refunds:

    Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 business days.
